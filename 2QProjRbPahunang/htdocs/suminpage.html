Sumin (수민)

  • Sumin is the oldest member in STAYC.
  • Her object in the group is ‘water’.
  • Her roommate in the dorm is Yoon.
  • She is a former Plan A Entertainment trainee.
  • Sumin made an appearance in Huh Gak‘s MV for The Last Night.
  • Sumin trained for 5 years and 6 months.
  • She was born and is from Pohang, North Gyeongsang in South Korea.
  • Her nicknames are: BaeSum and Sum.
  • Sumin used to train with the members of WEEEKLY.
  • She has an older sister.
